Coffee break with Posteljooni

Overview

The Problem

Loneliness is a serious social issue in Finland, particularly for the elderly. Loneliness not only contributes to major health problem, but also threats the elderly's lives. There is a huge need on having a frending to talk to. While Posti has a home service department, it needs a service to change the company image.

6 sprints

My Role

User Research, Ideation, Iterative Design, Graphic Design

Results

Posti representatives were interested in our idea, and kept our team in touch for further development.

Solution

Postmen utilise the legal 15-min coffeebreak during their daily work to have a small talk with the elderly and enjoy coffee together.

User Interview

Orignally, the idea was based on the understanding of the loneliness issue in the society. We know that there is a huge need on having a friend to talk to. To validated this idea, we went through multiple channels: Posti Kotipalvelut, Posti couriers and supervisor, and Helsinki Missio. Specifically, Helsinki Missios told us that they were lack of volunteers to provide the friend services. So many people in the city need this service which has already out of their capacity.

  • Helsinki Missio: " We are lack of volunteers to provide the friend service." --- There are many people in this city needing this friend service. However, Helsinki Missio has already out of their capacity.
  • Posti Kotipalvelut: "I really like this idea. Posti also offers a service that take the elderly to the destinations nearby during the winter. The elders love this service very much, but Posti ceased this service."
  • Postman:"We often have small chats with people with delivery. And our work is repetitive and lonely. I'd love to talk with people." --- Postmen has tight schedule but they still need social life.

Marketing Video

To sell our idea, we made a video to make Posti and the audience recognize the value of this service. We also would like to show the huge potential of home services in the market. There are a lot of people eager to have it, more than we expected.

Reflection

For this service design, we require quite a lot of support from Posti side. It would be better if we could conduct a more comprehensive user interview to understand courier's feelings and opinions towards this service. The essence of this idea is to connect people together as well as increase the reputation of Posti.

If the service need increases, we can develop an app for 'chat reservation'.
