Frenoor - Food sharing app

Overview

The Problem

Some people love cooking, and people who are too busy to cook. Frenoor is designed to connect these two groups of people and makes home cooking as a business so that everyone can have affordable and healthy meals.

My Role

UX Design, User Research

Timeline

August to Feb 2018

My Process

Paper prototyping -> Hi-fidelity protyotyping -> Usability Testing -> Iterative Design

Tool Used

Sketch, Principle

Solution

The app is designed to be simple and intuitive for most users. With the least clicks, they could get all the information they would like to know and order their desired homemade meals.

When we talk of home, it is always sweet and warm. The colour 'orange' is the same color as the sun, so it is chosen to be the primary colour. Grey and white are used to balance orange. And green is in contrast. Meanwhile, it indicates healthy as well.

Users can quickly browse the meal offerings nearby, and choose the meal he/she likes. The ingredients of the meal are clearly listed. Users are free to add more portions and leave a message to the home-made food cook.

Logo

For the App logo, I have come up with many ideas. Since our app is originated from spreading the love by home cooking. I tried combining little cute hearts with kitchen-related symbols such as fork and knife, plot, pan, and so forth.

Finally, our team tended to favor the pan with heart. Thus, I developed the initial simple pan to a pan house which can also pop out cute little hearts. These hearts also relate to wisps of smoke in the real life. In brief, this symbol perfectly combined two keywords, love, and home cooking together.

Reflection

The initial design of the app is to have two versions: one for the customer side, another for the business side. Here I only display the customer side prototypes, the business side prototypes were not ready.

Moreover, this app is no longer in development. I left the startup since I would like to pursue a Master's degree in 2018.

All in all, I am still grateful for this experience and enjoyed working with my team members. They are great people with talented skills. This app has its potential in the market.
