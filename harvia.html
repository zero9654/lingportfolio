Soulna - Hack the Sauna Experience

Overview

The Problem

Hack the sauna experience is a hackathon organized by Ultrahack. Harvia is a global leader in the sauna & spa industry. This time, they gave the challenge to integrate the modern technology into traditional sauna. Harvia looks forward to explore the future sauna experience from both the multisensory aspect and the wellness aspect.

My Role

UI Design, Ideation

Results

We won the hackathon with the prize of 5000 euro.

Solution

We offer a combined solution including smart sauna and health data visualization. We wish to empower sauna with more personalised features and safety consideration, so we introduce the smartband for data tracking. With a certain amount of data, smart sauna would be able to learn from the historical data and suggest appropriate temperature and humidity to endusers. The health data visualization is a dashboard available on the web. Users can easily see their body indicators, skin condition, sleep condition, etc so that this can help them understand the effect of having sauna regularly.

Tool Used

Figma, Pencil & Paper

Timeline

Nov 16-18 2021

Value proposition

For customers, our solution quantifies and visualizes the sauna experience. We provide personalised sauna experience.

For Harvia customers, we enlarge the customer group by integrating smartband into sanua experience and talomg beauty lovers into consideration. With smartband and dashboard, we strengthen user stickiness.
