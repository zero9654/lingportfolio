I'MPACT

Overview

The Problem

VTT faces the ongoing challenge of retaining talented employees, leading to high turnover rates and potential negative impacts on productivity and morale.

Results

We won the challenge of VTT. We introduced an entryview to understand the potention career developement of talents and a I'MPACT meter to visualise employee's achievements and their impact in the fields.

My Role

Problem validation, Solution discovery, Web design

Tool Used

Figma, Google slides

Timeline

8 - 9 Oct 2022, DASH Design hackathon

Solutions

  1. A web application that displays all services in a gallery view
  2. AI-powered chatbot to provide 24/7 support
  3. Sanoo booth that provides a private, safe and cozy zone for all young people to openly discuss their strugglings

Challenges of VTT

1. How to make sure people have the right perception of the position while applying?
2. How to meet young people’s expectations and motivate them while working at VTT?
3. How can an employee avoid doubt about making an actual change in the world?

For some young people, they may feel uncomfortable to express their strugglings openly. Based on discussion with mentors, we understood that the huge sucess of Sekasin also thanks to the collaborations with influencers and yle. So, this shall be taken into consideration how we can promote our services smoothly to avoid young people feel pressure or ashamed.

Future vision

The concept of Sanoo booth will first have a pilot testing for a week, and then scale up to the nationwide roadtrip. In the near future, we also have the vision to make the Sanoo booth serve a broader group of people. All residents, no matter what age, would be welcomed to our Sanoo booth to express their feelings and have a warm chat. We also have an idea to expand the Sanoo booth to accommodate a family which is also worth validating through the future pilot testing.

We are always improving, developing, and evolving together with our young people. Our vision is to create an open, warm and secure environment where young people could get their support, parents could get guidance, volunteers could gain information on lending a helping hand, and partners and donors could also get access to realize their value.
